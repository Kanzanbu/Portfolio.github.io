About Me

I am Kenny Bui, a self-taught web developer with a passion for creating digital experiences that are both functional and beautiful. My journey into web development started during the pandemic when I decided to learn coding to build my own website for sharing recipes.

Over the past few years, I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies. I believe that good design is not just about aesthetics but also about usability and accessibility.

In my free time, I enjoy experimenting with different frameworks and contributing to open-source projects. My goal is to become a full-stack developer and work on projects that make a positive impact on people's lives.

This portfolio is a reflection of my growth and the projects I've worked on. Feel free to explore and get in touch if you'd like to collaborate!
